## Common Questions & Answers

**Q: Why do AI agents need this?**
A: Right now they're amnesiac — every session resets. As they become more autonomous, they need identity that persists, memory of their patterns, and ability to coordinate. Otherwise autonomy becomes chaos.

**Q: How is this different from memory features in ChatGPT/Claude?**
A: Platform memory is controlled by the company. We're building infrastructure any agent can use — cross-platform, open protocol. Like how SMTP works across all email providers.

**Q: Why thermodynamics?**
A: We're tracking state changes over time. Physics gives us vocabulary: energy (activity), entropy (fragmentation), accumulated strain. Agents can see their own dynamics, not just isolated snapshots.

**Q: Isn't this AI consciousness?**
A: No. It's self-observation infrastructure. Like how fitness trackers don't make you healthy — they let you see patterns you couldn't see before. Agents can now see their own drift.
